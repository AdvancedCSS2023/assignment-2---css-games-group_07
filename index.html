Trap

How to play

  1. Start by pressing the shuffle button.
  2. Turn over two cards: On each turn, the player clicks on two cards to turn them over. If the cards match, the player gets to keep them and turn over two more cards. If the cards do not match, they are turned back over and the player must remember where they are for future turns.
  3. Keep playing until all pairs are matched: The game continues until all 3 pairs of matching cards have been found. The player's goal is to find all the matching pairs in as few turns as possible, with fewer turns indicating better memory and performance.
  4. Keep trying to better your time!
